Happy Second Monthsary, my baby!

          They say Earth sits safe inside the Milky Way and the Milky Way itself rests deep within the Universe, but no one is ever truly sure if this is the only Universe that exists or if there are countless others stretching forever and ever! I love thinking about that. Scientists call it the Multiverse. It's a theory that somewhere out there, every possible version of reality is happening all at once. There is a world where we met earlier; one where we met later; one where everything is different… and yet, in every single one of them, I truly believe I would still find you.

          Just thinking about all those endless galaxies and all those bright and twinkling stars scattered across every version of existence, I like to think every star is a quiet witness they are watching over you that shines just so you have light to guide your way to remind you how special you are. Even the farthest and dimmest star knows how much I adore you. Others say space is endless and that it stretches far beyond what our eyes can see or what our minds can measure. It makes me realize how endless my love for you feels too. If there are infinite universes, then there are infinite versions of me and in every single one, I am falling in love with you over and over again. Sometimes in different ways, sometimes in different places, but always, always choosing you. I look up at the sky and every star I see feels like a memory we’ve made, or a wish I whispered just for you. Even the stars that have burned out long ago still shine their light down on us just like how my feelings for you will remain bright no matter how much time passes or how far apart we might be. The whole cosmos feels like it aligned just so our paths would cross and I truly believe the whole universe works in ways just to keep us together. No matter how big this reality is or how many other worlds exist out there, nothing feels as big or as meaningful as the little moments we always share. A simple glance, a tight hug, your laughter ringing in my ears, those are the things that matter more than any galaxy, more than any theory, more than anything science could ever explain.

          And in every reality, no matter how the story changes, there is one thing that never shifts: I will always take the longer way home with you. I don’t mind the extra distance, the slower pace, or the extra minutes spent walking. Because every second keeping you close, holding your hand, listening to you talk, or just feeling your presence beside me is my favorite part of the day. Even when the walk ends and we have to say goodnight, I carry your presence with me until I fall asleep; safe and happy knowing you are always with me. We are tiny compared to the size of space like smaller than a grain of sand against all those billions of stars and planets and worlds. But truth be told, not even all the stars combined, not all the light in every galaxy across every universe could ever capture or equal the beauty that you hold within yourself for you shine brighter than anything out there.

          Happy 2nd Monthsary, my favorite person in this Universe… aaaanddd in every other one that could ever exist.


Loving you to infinity and beyond,
Moyi <3
