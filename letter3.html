Happy Third Monthsary, Nini!




          Sometimes I think about how we found each other and it feels like something more than just a chance. It's as if someone guided our paths to cross; not in a hurry, not by force, but gently at just the right time. What started as small moments, quiet glances, and soft little hopes slowly grew into something real and steady.


          I remember how we took it one day at a time, a bit confused, a bit unsure on where this would lead to at first but willing to try to find where it goes. Every conversation, every laugh, every time I am near you makes it clearer: this is exactly what I've been waiting for. It's that kind of love that doesn't need to rush. It just unfolds naturally like it was always meant to be. It feels like our hearts were tied long before we met bound by something invisible but strong. No pressure, no expectations; just two people finding their way to each other and choosing to stay. And yes, it hasn't always been perfect. There have been times when we didn't see eye to eye, when we misunderstood each other, and when small disagreements turned into bigger feelings. We've had our share of fights, our moments of silence, and times when things felt heavy or difficult. But even then, even when we are upset or far apart in thoughts, one thing never changes: my love for you never gets less. It doesn't fade, it doesn't weaken, and it never goes away. If anything, every challenge we face only shows how strong what we share really is.


          Thank you for picking me and for making every single month feel warm and meaningful. I promise to keep cherishing what we have—to keep caring, to keep hoping, and to love you deeper each day. To many more memories and a love that only grows stronger as time goes by.


Yours always,
Moyiyi <3
